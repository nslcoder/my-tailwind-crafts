What's the Point?

Have you ever had that moment where you stare at your screen, just trying to think of a good headline for your email, website or product... but nothing really happens? Wouldn't it be great to just skip that?

This book was initially a Google Spreadsheet (Gijs and Danny) created for our own projects. We were sick and tired of those days we kept looking at a blank screen, unable to come up with any headlines, titles or subject lines.

We a/b tested the new headlines on the website of our clients and got amazing results. Some headlines even doubled the initial conversion rates!
